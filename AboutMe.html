ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is John Sven Gorres. I was born on June 6, 2006, in Tondo, Manila. Currently, I am studying at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila (PLM) as a first-year college student, pursuing a degree in Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship. Even though I am enrolled in this course, I don't feel fully connected to it and plan to shift to Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) in my second year, as that is where my passion truly lies.

In my free time, I have various hobbies that keep me active and engaged. I enjoy playing video games, which has been a big part of my life, and exercising regularly, as it helps me stay healthy and energized. Sleeping and eating are also simple pleasures I love. But what I enjoy most of all is coding. I’ve recently developed a deep interest in programming and find joy in creating and solving problems through code. I know that these skills will be important for my future career, especially when I transition to IT.

Although I don’t have any major achievements in school yet, I am determined to study hard and do my best in everything. I know how important education is, not only for me but also for my family, and I want to succeed to help them in the future. It’s my goal to graduate, and I am willing to work hard to make sure that happens.
